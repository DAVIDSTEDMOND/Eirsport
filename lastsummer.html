THE CELEBRATION OF LIFE.

I designed a title sequence(visuals and music) for a fourth year film student. The movie was titled "Last Summer". His movie is about a man who wants to recreate his dying fiances favourite memory, a Christmas night when they both danced together.

I used and ornament of a woman to represent his fiance. The ornament is rotating to create the illusion that it is dancing. The smokey dark space makes the motion appear dreamlike.

I made a digipak case for the DVD because I thought that it was more appropriate to the movie. I printed on satin paper.
